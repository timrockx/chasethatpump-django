Dumbbells

Dumbells should be a staple in any workout routine. They are easily accessible and incredibly multi-functional, from a traditional dumbbell press, to bulgarian split squats.

Cables

Cable machines are excellent for accessory exercises to go alongside compound lifts. With constant tension throughout the ROM, they are ideal for hypertrophy. Shown above are cable chest flys.

Barbells

Barbells are often used for compound lifts as they are easier to progressively overload with weight, in addition to greater stability than dumbbells. Common exercises performed with barbells include the bench press, deadlift, and overhead press.
